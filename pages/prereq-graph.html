Prerequisite graph, D3-driven

Test page — not linked from Classes

One combined view centered on a module: its prerequisite chain flows IN from the left, converging into the module in the middle, then the module fans OUT to the right toward everything that lists it as a prerequisite (Boston Green Line style). It's the two separate directions from the earlier D3 demo — the backward "what this needs" map and the forward "what this leads to" fan-out — stitched into a single graph that shares the one center station. Built as its own page on purpose, so reworking the layout here can't break that still-working demo. Everything else carries over: real D3-computed pixel coordinates (same components, so city theme and dark mode still work for free), rounded 90° bends, ghost routing nodes, drag-to-pan. Click any other station to re-center the whole graph on it.
